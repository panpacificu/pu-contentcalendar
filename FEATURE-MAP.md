# Feature Map

## Retained from PU Content Planner v1.2.0

- Panpacific University visual system and responsive layout
- Fixed poster/photo and video/short-form publication slots
- Monthly and weekly calendars
- Weekly slot-based drag and drop
- Quick status controls
- Hide Week / Show Week
- Floating Manila date and time
- Floating refresh and changelog controls
- Duplicate-save protection concept

## Adapted from Planora

- Supabase PostgreSQL data layer
- Account authentication and persistent sessions
- Realtime synchronization and refresh fallback
- Workspace membership roles
- Publication channel management
- Search and multi-filter toolbar
- Idea, Created, Scheduled, and Posted workflow
- Rich post fields: platform, caption, notes, category, channel
- Account/profile settings
- Optimistic drag and status updates with rollback
- Sync-status feedback and keyboard shortcuts

## Added specifically for this release

- Detailed Daily view with every publication slot
- Drag-and-drop rescheduling in Month, Week, and Day views
- Admin, Editor, and Viewer RLS policies
- Automatic activity logging
- Legacy Google Sheet CSV conversion and import workflow

- Shift + Drag duplicates a card into a new date or time slot without removing the original.
